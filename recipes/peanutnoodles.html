Peanut Noodles

Description

This is another dish that is very easy to make! The noodle type can play a big role in the flavor so we recommend you experiment with different noodles. A bite of delishg every time!

Ingredients

  • Noodles of choice
  • Peanut butter
  • Sesame oil
  • Sriracha
  • Sesame seeds
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions
  2. Heat peanut butter over high heat and add soy sauce
  3. Whisk thoroughly while combining all ingredients
  4. Add noodles to sauce and garnish with sesame seeds!
