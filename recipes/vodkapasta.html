Vodka Pasta

Description

This dish is very easy to make and is a crowd favorite. It is a comfort food in my home and I get asked tomake it all the time. You won't need many ingredients, and it is able to be prepared rather quickly. Please see the following ingredients list and I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • Vodka
  • Heavy Cream
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil and cook pasta
  2. Simmer vodka in pan on high heat for 10 mintues
  3. Mix in cooked pasta, tomato paste, cream and fresh shaved parmesan
