Chicken Tenders

Description

A classic! Chicken tenders with an Italian twist. Everyone loves them, especially Dwayne.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Seasoning
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breasts to desired size
  2. Coat tenders in flour mixture
  3. Dip in whisked eggs
  4. Dip in seasonings, breadcrumbs and parmesan
  5. Bake for 20 minutes at 400!
